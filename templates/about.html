The Call Trends Android App:

Download the Call Trends Android App in one of three ways:

  1. Download CallTrends to your Android powered phone by launching the android market in the application menu, searching for "CallTrends" -- install and done.
  2. Download the app using the Barcode Scanner on your phone and the QR Code found by clicking this link.
  3. Copy & paste: http://calltrends.appspot.com/download

The story:

A Googler wrote MailTrends -- an awesome application that allows you to look at trend data from your Gmail account(s). I love looking at trends, I love graphs and charts so I tried putting something similar together for phone calls.
